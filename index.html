Hall of Fame

The immortals who shaped sports entertainment forever

Inducted: 2022

The Undertaker

"The Deadman" / "The Phenom"

Mark William Calaway dominated WWE for over 30 years. His legendary WrestleMania streak of 21-0 remains one of the greatest achievements in wrestling history.

7x World Champion WM Streak: 21-0
Inducted: 2009

Stone Cold Steve Austin

"The Texas Rattlesnake"

The face of the Attitude Era. Austin 3:16 changed the wrestling industry forever. His rivalry with Vince McMahon is the greatest storyline in WWE history.

6x World Champion 3x Royal Rumble
Inducted: 2008

The Rock

"The People's Champion" / "The Great One"

Dwayne Johnson electrified millions with his charisma and in-ring ability. Transitioned from WWE's biggest star to Hollywood's highest-paid actor.

10x World Champion 2x Royal Rumble
Inducted: 2011

Shawn Michaels

"The Heartbreak Kid" / "Mr. WrestleMania"

Widely regarded as the greatest in-ring performer ever. HBK's matches at WrestleMania are considered masterpieces of storytelling and athleticism.

4x World Champion 2x Royal Rumble
Inducted: 2019

Triple H

"The Game" / "The King of Kings"

Paul Levesque dominated as a wrestler and now leads WWE as Chief Content Officer. DX, Evolution, and The Authority defined multiple eras of WWE.

14x World Champion WWE CCO
Inducted: 2008 & 2012

Ric Flair

"The Nature Boy" — Wooooo!

The stylin', profilin', limousine ridin', jet flyin' son of a gun. 16-time World Champion and the standard by which all others are measured.

16x World Champion 2x HOF Inductee
Inducted: 2005

Hulk Hogan

"The Immortal" / "Hulkamania"

The man who made wrestling mainstream. Hulkamania ran wild through the 1980s and 90s, making WWE a global phenomenon. Body slammed Andre at WM III.

6x World Champion Hulkamania Era
Inducted: 2006 & 2019

Bret "The Hitman" Hart

"The Best There Is, Was, and Ever Will Be"

Technical excellence personified. The Excellence of Execution brought mat-based wrestling to the forefront and inspired generations of wrestlers worldwide.

7x World Champion Hart Foundation
Inducted: 2012

Edge

"The Rated-R Superstar" / "The Ultimate Opportunist"

Adam Copeland overcame career-ending injuries to return and prove he's one of the greatest. First Money in the Bank winner and multi-time champion.

11x World Champion 2x Royal Rumble

Current Superstars

The present and future of World Wrestling Entertainment

SmackDown

Roman Reigns

"The Tribal Chief" / "The Head of the Table"

The longest reigning Universal Champion in history (1,316 days). Roman Reigns redefined what it means to be a top star with his Bloodline faction.

6x World Champion The Bloodline
SmackDown

Cody Rhodes

"The American Nightmare"

Finished the story at WrestleMania 40 by defeating Roman Reigns. Son of Dusty Rhodes, Cody left WWE, built AEW, returned, and captured the Undisputed Championship.

Undisputed Champion 2x Rumble Winner
Raw

Seth Rollins

"The Visionary" / "The Architect"

One of the most complete performers in WWE today. From The Shield to singles dominance, Rollins delivers show-stealing matches at every event.

7x World Champion Mr. MITB
Raw

GUNTHER

"The Ring General"

The longest reigning Intercontinental Champion in history. GUNTHER brings a hard-hitting, European style that has made him one of the most feared competitors.

World Heavyweight Champ King of the Ring
Raw

Rhea Ripley

"Mami" / "The Eradicator"

The most dominant woman in WWE today. Rhea's combination of power, charisma, and intimidation has made her the face of the women's division.

Women's World Champ Judgment Day
SmackDown

Bianca Belair

"The EST of WWE"

The Strongest, Fastest, Toughest, Roughest, and the EST of everything. Bianca's athleticism is unmatched and she's headlined multiple WrestleManias.

3x Champion Royal Rumble 2021
Raw

Drew McIntyre

"The Scottish Warrior"

From being released to winning the WWE Championship at WrestleMania 36. Drew's comeback story is one of the greatest redemption arcs in wrestling.

2x WWE Champion Royal Rumble 2020
SmackDown

LA Knight

"The Megastar" / "YEAH!"

The most over superstar in WWE with crowd reactions rivaling Attitude Era levels. LA Knight's mic skills and charisma have made him a fan-favorite phenomenon.

US Champion Most Popular 2024
Raw

Jey Uso

"Main Event Jey" / "YEET!"

From tag team legend to singles breakout star. Jey Uso's journey from The Bloodline to becoming one of the most beloved superstars has been phenomenal.

IC Champion The Bloodline
